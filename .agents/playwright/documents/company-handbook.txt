# TechnoVault Inc. Employee Handbook
Version 3.7 - Effective January 2024

## Company Overview

TechnoVault Inc. was founded in 2018 by Dr. Elena Marchetti and Marcus Wong in Boulder, Colorado. The company specializes in quantum-resistant cryptography solutions for enterprise clients.

## Key Facts

- Headquarters: 742 Quantum Drive, Boulder, CO 80301
- Employee Count: 847 employees as of Q4 2023
- Annual Revenue: $127 million USD (FY 2023)
- Stock Ticker: TVLT (NASDAQ)

## Core Values

1. **Integrity First**: We never compromise on security or ethics
2. **Innovation Through Collaboration**: Great ideas come from diverse teams
3. **Customer Obsession**: Every decision starts with the customer
4. **Sustainable Growth**: We build for the long term

## Benefits Summary

### Health Insurance
All full-time employees receive comprehensive health coverage through BlueCross BlueShield. The company covers 85% of premium costs for employees and 70% for dependents.

### Retirement
TechnoVault offers a 401(k) plan with a company match of 6% of salary. Vesting is immediate for the employee contribution and follows a 3-year cliff vesting schedule for employer contributions.

### Paid Time Off
- Vacation: 20 days per year (accrued monthly)
- Sick Leave: 12 days per year
- Personal Days: 3 days per year
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks paid for all new parents

### Professional Development
Each employee has an annual learning budget of $3,500 for courses, conferences, and certifications.

## Office Hours

Standard office hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM Mountain Time, Monday through Friday. The company observes 11 paid holidays per year.

## Emergency Contact

For security incidents, contact the Security Operations Center (SOC) at extension 4911 or email security@technovault.example.com
